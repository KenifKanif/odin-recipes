Delicious Description

I love these little energy bites because they are easy to make with simply pantry ingredients that I almost always have on hand. They are super easy to mix up and roll into cute little energy balls.

They are the perfect make-ahead recipe to keep on hand for healthy snacking or breakfasts. And most importantly, they are just irresistibly good.

Recipe

  • Old-fashioned oats
  • Shredded coconut
  • Creamy peanut butter
  • Ground flaxseed
  • Semisweet chocolate chips
  • Honey
  • Vanilla extract
  • Chia seeds(optional)

Steps

  1. Stir everything together
  2. Chill
  3. Roll into balls
  4. Serve
